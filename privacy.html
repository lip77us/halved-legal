Privacy Policy

Halved
Effective date: May 30, 2026
Last updated: May 30, 2026

Halved (“the App”) is operated by Paul Lipkin (“we,” “us,” or “our”). This Privacy Policy explains what information the App collects, how we use it, and the choices you have. The App helps golfers set up casual rounds and track friendly golf games and scoring among the players in their group.

By creating an account or using the App, you agree to the practices described in this policy.

1. Information We Collect

Account information

When an account is created for you, we store:

  • A username and a password (the password is stored only in a securely hashed form — we never store or see your raw password).
  • Optionally, your email address and your first and last name, if provided.
  • The group (“account”) you belong to, which lets the people in your golf group share players, rounds, and games.

Player profile

A player profile may include:

  • Your name and a short display name (e.g., initials).
  • Optionally, an email address and phone number, if provided.
  • Your golf handicap index.
  • A men’s / women’s designation, used only to pick the default set of tees when setting up a round.

Golf activity

As you use the App, we store the golf data you and your group create:

  • Rounds, courses, tees, and the players grouped into each foursome.
  • Hole-by-hole scores.
  • Games played and their results, including the per-point or per-hole stakes you set for friendly games and the resulting tallies.

Information stored only on your device

  • An authentication token that keeps you signed in between sessions.
  • A local copy of recent scores so you can keep entering scores when your phone is temporarily offline; this syncs to our server when a connection returns.
  • Your personal in-app preferences (such as score-entry display options).

2. Information We Do Not Collect

We want to be clear about what the App does not do:

  • The App does not access your device location, camera, photos, contacts, or microphone.
  • The App does not contain any advertising, and we do not sell your information.
  • The App does not include third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking software.
  • We do not track you across other companies’ apps or websites.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the information above only to operate the App, specifically to:

  • Authenticate you and keep your account secure.
  • Set up rounds, calculate handicaps, and score the golf games you play.
  • Share players, rounds, and game results among the members of your golf group.
  • Maintain, troubleshoot, and improve the App.

The friendly-game stakes recorded in the App are for scoring and settling up informally among friends. The App does not process payments and is not a gambling or money-transfer service.

4. How Information Is Shared

We do not sell your personal information. We share it only as follows:

  • Members of your golf group. Players, rounds, scores, and game results are visible to the other members of the account (group) you belong to — that is the core purpose of the App.
  • Hosting / infrastructure provider. The App’s data is stored on cloud infrastructure operated by our hosting provider, which processes data solely on our behalf to run the service.
  • Golf course information service. To help you add a course, the App may request public course and tee data from a third-party golf course database. We send only the course search terms needed for that lookup; we do not send your personal information to that service.
  • Legal reasons. We may disclose information if required by law or to protect the rights, safety, or property of our users or others.

5. Data Retention and Account Deletion

You can delete your account at any time from within the App (Settings → Delete Account).

When you delete your account:

  • Your login (username and password) is permanently removed and you are signed out. You will no longer be able to sign in.
  • Personal details on your player profile — your name, email, and phone number — are removed or replaced with an anonymous placeholder.
  • Because your past scores are part of shared scorecards that belong to other golfers in your group, those historical game records may be retained in an anonymized form (no longer associated with your name or contact details) so that other players’ round histories remain intact.

If you would like help deleting your account or have questions about your data, contact us at the email address below.

6. Data Security

We take reasonable measures to protect your information. Passwords are stored only as salted hashes, data is transmitted over encrypted (HTTPS) connections, and access to stored data is restricted. No method of transmission or storage is completely secure, however, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

7. Children’s Privacy

The App is intended for a general audience and is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us and we will delete it.

8. International Users

The App is operated from the United States, and your information is stored and processed there. If you use the App from outside the United States, you understand that your information will be transferred to and processed in the United States.

9. Your Choices and Rights

You can review and update your player profile within the App, and you can delete your account as described in Section 5. Depending on where you live, you may have additional rights regarding your personal information (such as the right to access or delete it). To exercise any such rights, contact us at the email address below.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will revise the “Last updated” date at the top of this page. Significant changes will be reflected here, and your continued use of the App after an update means you accept the revised policy.
