Common questions

  • How do I delete my account? Open the app, go to Settings → Delete Account, and confirm. This permanently removes your login; past scores are kept anonymously as part of other players' round history.
  • How do I set up a game? Start a round, pick one or more games (Skins, Nassau, Sixes, Points 5-3-1, Match Play, and more), assign players and tees, then enter scores hole by hole. Halved handles the handicaps, live results, and the settle-up at the end.
  • Forgot your password? An account admin can reset member passwords from Manage Members. If you're the admin and need help, email us at the address above.
  • Privacy: see our Privacy Policy.

Halved is built for friendly play among friends. It tracks informal stakes for scoring purposes only and does not process payments.
